## 📈 1. Key Betting Data & Trends (2026 Season)

### Public Money vs. Sharps (Market Alpha)
- **Fading the Public**: Track the "Consensus" (percentage of bets on favorites vs. underdogs). Inflated lines are often found where public sentiment is high for popular teams.
- **Sharp Money Discrepancy**: Identify "Sharp Moves" by analyzing the delta between **Bet Volume %** (Public) and **Handle/Money %** (Sharps). High money on low bet volume indicates professional sharp money.
- **Moneyline Base Rates**: MLB favorites historically win 58%-62% of games. Do not bet exclusively on favorites due to high "juice" (Vig).

### Spread & Totals
- **Run-Line Splits (+1.5/-1.5)**: Evaluate value on the spread compared to the moneyline, especially when a dominant pitcher is involved.
- **Over/Under Record Tracking**: Monitor team-specific performance against the total runs line to identify consistent pitching staff over/under performance.

## 🧬 2. Critical Predictive Statistics

### The Pitching Anchor
- **Starting Pitcher Form**: The primary variable in MLB betting.
- **Metrics**: 
  - **K/9** (Strikeouts per 9 innings)
  - **Pitcher Money Won/Lost** (Actual profitability per start)
  - **Recent Form** (Last 3-5 outings)

### Advanced Valuation Metrics
- **Elo Foundations**: See [elo_foundations.md](file:///c:/Users/clear/MLB/directives/elo_foundations.md) for theory and implementation.
- **Sabermetrics Foundations**: See [sabermetrics_foundations.md](file:///c:/Users/clear/MLB/directives/sabermetrics_foundations.md) for historical context.
- **2026 Season Context**: See [mlb_2026_season_context.md](file:///c:/Users/clear/MLB/directives/mlb_2026_season_context.md) for live situational data.
- **Full Glossary**: See [mlb_statistics_glossary.md](file:///c:/Users/clear/MLB/directives/mlb_statistics_glossary.md) for specific definitions.
- **Pythagorean Expectation (ExW-L)**: 
  - Formula: $\text{Runs Scored}^2 / (\text{Runs Scored}^2 + \text{Runs Allowed}^2)$
  - Purpose: Identifies "lucky" teams poised for negative regression or "unlucky" teams poised for an upturn.
- **Relative Power Index (RPI)**: Weighted ranking of Team Win% (25%), Opponent Win% (50%), and Opponent's Opponent Win% (25%).
- **Statcast Intelligence**: Monitor **xBA** (Expected Batting Average), **Exit Velocity**, and **Barrel Percentage** via Baseball Savant.
- **OBP (On-Base Percentage)**: The most critical team batting metric for predicting wins (indicator of patience and efficiency).

## 🌤️ 3. Essential Situational Factors

### The Environment
- **Park Factors & Weather**:
  - **Wrigley Field Context**: Wind speed and direction (In/Out) drastically shift total run expectations.
  - **Venue Alpha**: Home/Road splits can show significant performance discrepancies.
- **Umpire Analytics**: Home plate umpire strike zone bias (Tight vs. Large) directly correlates to O/U outcomes.

### Operational Context
- **Bullpen Utilization**: Evaluate reliever fatigue (back-to-back games) and depth.
- **Situational Momentum**: Identify high-stress/rivalry series vs. travel-fatigue scenarios.
