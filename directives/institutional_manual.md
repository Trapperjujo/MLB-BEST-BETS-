## 📐 Pillar 1: The +EV Philosophy (Expected Value)

In sports betting, we do not bet on *who will win*. We bet on **price discrepancies**.

### What is +EV?
Positive Expected Value (+EV) occurs when our model's probability of an outcome is higher than the bookmaker's implied probability.
- **Model Win%**: 60% (Implied Odds: -150)
- **Market Odds**: +110 (Implied Win%: 47.6%)

## 🎲 Pillar 2: Kelly Criterion (Bankroll Management)

The single biggest reason bettors fail is poor staking. We use a **Fractional Kelly Criterion** to ensure you never go bust.

### The Formula
`Stake = (p * b - q) / b`
- `p` = Probability of winning
- `q` = Probability of losing (1 - p)
- `b` = Decimal odds minus 1

### Why Fractional Kelly?
The full Kelly criterion can be volatile. We recommend a **0.25x (Quarter Kelly)** reduction factor. This preserves your bankroll during cold streaks while still capturing exponential growth during winning runs.
